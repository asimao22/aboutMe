About Me

Hello! I'm Alison Simao. I am a 22 year old college student currently studying Graphic Design at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. I've lived in Rhode Island my entire life, and was lucky to grow up in a home with a lot of family around.
I have loved art ever since I was a kid. Some of my earliest memories are of being at home watching Bob Ross on my tv creating paintings that blew my mind. I knew from that point I wanted to somehow create something of that magnitude myself. Of course, three year old Alison could only scribble at that point. As I grew older, I began to experiment and dive into various mediums and styles of art. I am a designer, a photographer, and a painter.
In my free time, I enjoy playing guitar, traveling around wherever I can, and lately, teaching myself about live sound production (I am a massive music nerd).

Whatever allows me to express creativity, makes me feel free.
